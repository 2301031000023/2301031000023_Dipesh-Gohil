Martina Markulin recommended A changemaker's guide to the future :
Imagine a world without resource scarcity, where our behaviour doesn't have a negative impact on our climate, where waste doesn't exist, where sustainability and growth are each other's prerequisites. To us, this world is real. More than ever, it is time to change the sustainability narrative along with the structure of our modern society. Because the world as we know it is changing. Digitalisation, globalisation, urbanisation, climate change, the fourth industrial revolution.
